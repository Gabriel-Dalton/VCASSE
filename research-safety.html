Key Research Vectors

01 / LOGIC

Formal Verification

Developing rigorous mathematical proofs to ensure AI behavior remains within strictly defined safety envelopes under all operating conditions.

02 / INFRA

Robust Architectures

Engineering neural networks with inherent resilience to adversarial perturbations and out-of-distribution environmental shifts.

03 / METRICS

Risk Assessment

Quantifying existential and operational risks through continuous simulation of edge-case scenarios and multi-agent friction.
