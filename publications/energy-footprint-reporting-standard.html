Energy Footprint Reporting: A Starter Standard

A baseline reporting format for teams that want to disclose AI energy impact with minimal overhead.

SustainabilityNov 19, 20256 min read

Energy reporting in AI remains inconsistent across institutions, making comparison and accountability difficult.

This starter standard recommends reporting by workload class, region, model family, and quarterly trend, with confidence notes where estimation is required.

Standardized disclosures can strengthen policy discussions and improve public trust without imposing excessive burden on smaller teams.
