Participatory Ethics in Municipal AI Decisions

A framework for bringing resident voices into high-impact municipal AI decisions before deployment.

EthicsJan 31, 20268 min read

Public-sector AI ethics reviews are strongest when affected communities shape criteria and trade-offs early.

VCASSE recommends publishing plain-language use-case briefs, running structured listening sessions, and recording decision changes tied to public feedback.

This participatory model improves legitimacy and identifies real-world risks that internal review teams often miss.
