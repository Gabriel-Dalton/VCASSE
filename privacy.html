Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

The Vancouver Centre for AI Safety, Sustainability, and Ethics ("VCASSE," "we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or engage with our research programs.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways, including:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number, institutional affiliation, and other contact information you voluntarily provide when submitting forms, applying for programs, or contacting us.
  • Usage Data: Browser type, operating system, access times, pages viewed, and the referring URL. This data is collected automatically through cookies and similar technologies.
  • Research Data: If you participate in our research programs, we may collect data related to your research activities, publications, and professional background.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To process applications for residency programs, grants, and collaborations
  • To communicate with you about our research, events, and publications
  • To improve our website and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights
  • To conduct anonymized research on website usage patterns

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Research Partners: With your consent, we may share relevant information with academic and industry partners for collaborative research purposes.
  • Service Providers: We may share information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website and conducting our programs, subject to confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of VCASSE, our users, or others.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit certain functionality of our website.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access and receive a copy of your personal data
  • The right to rectify inaccurate or incomplete data
  • The right to request deletion of your personal data
  • The right to restrict or object to processing
  • The right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@vcasse.org.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable laws and regulations. Research-related data may be retained for longer periods in accordance with academic data management standards.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:
