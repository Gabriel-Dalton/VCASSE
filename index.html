Who We Are

VCASSE is a non-profit centre dedicated to ensuring that the development and deployment of AI systems benefits everyone — now and in the future.

Founded in Vancouver, BC, we bring together researchers, technologists, policymakers, and the public to address the most pressing challenges at the intersection of artificial intelligence, environmental sustainability, and social ethics.

We believe that safe and ethical AI is not just a technical problem — it is a social, environmental, and governance challenge that requires diverse voices and collaborative action.

Our Three Pillars

Everything we do is grounded in three interconnected areas that together shape the future of AI.

🛡️

AI Safety

We research and promote techniques to ensure AI systems behave reliably, predictably, and in alignment with human values — reducing the risk of unintended harm from automated decision-making.

🌱

Sustainability

We examine the environmental impact of AI — from the energy consumed by large models to the carbon footprint of data centres — and advocate for greener, more resource-efficient approaches.

⚖️

Ethics

We engage with questions of fairness, accountability, transparency, and privacy in AI systems, working to ensure these technologies serve the public interest and respect human rights.
