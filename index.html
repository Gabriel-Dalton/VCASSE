Safety, Sustainability, & Ethics

Vancouver's dedicated centre for responsible AI. We research, educate, and advocate across three pillars — because AI is moving fast, and the people it affects most deserve a seat at the table.

OUR FOCUS

Three Pillars of Research

Our work spans the critical intersections of AI safety, environmental sustainability, and ethical governance.

01

AI Safety

AI safety isn't just a concern for researchers at major labs. It's a concern for every person who interacts with AI — and that's increasingly everyone. We advocate for responsible development practices that prioritize long-term wellbeing over speed to market.

LEARN MORE
02

Sustainability

Data centres consume enormous amounts of energy and water. Training large language models has a measurable carbon footprint. Yet most AI discourse treats these costs as footnotes. We research the environmental impact of AI and advocate for efficiency standards.

LEARN MORE
03

Ethics

Ethics is where the hard questions live. Not "can we do this?" but "what happens when we do?" We examine the ethical boundaries of AI applications — from deepfakes and surveillance to algorithmic bias — and hold builders accountable.

ABOUT VCASSE

Our Mission

Our Mission

To position Vancouver as a leader in responsible AI by advancing public literacy, informing policy, and ensuring that safety, sustainability, and ethics are at the centre of how our community builds, uses, and governs artificial intelligence.

Our Vision

A Vancouver where every resident — from students to policymakers to developers — understands the impact of AI on their lives and has the tools to shape its future responsibly.
