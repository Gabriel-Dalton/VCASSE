PILLAR 01

AI Safety & Robustness

AI safety isn't just a concern for researchers at major labs. It's a concern for every person who interacts with AI, and that's increasingly everyone. Our safety work ensures AI systems are developed and used in ways that protect people, communities, and the natural world.

RESEARCH AREAS

Safety Focus

01.1

Responsible Development

Advocating for responsible development practices that prioritize long-term wellbeing over speed to market. The goal is an AI ecosystem that's safe for people, for communities, and for the planet.

  • DEVELOPMENT PRACTICES
  • LONG-TERM WELLBEING
01.2

Public Understanding

Ensuring consumers understand what "safe AI" actually means and what to demand from the companies building it. Safety is about what we build and how fast we build it.

  • CONSUMER EDUCATION
  • AI LITERACY
01.3

Future Generations

Researching how AI systems can be kept safe, legal, and socially responsible at a development pace that doesn't compromise future generations, including the children, wildlife, and ecosystems that don't get a vote.

  • INTERGENERATIONAL SAFETY
  • ECOLOGICAL RESPONSIBILITY
