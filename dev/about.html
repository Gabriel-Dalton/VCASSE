Our founding document

We are Vancouver's centre for responsible AI.

Built so the city's future with intelligent systems is one Vancouver helps decide. Not one it inherits.

What follows is VCASSE's constitution: the mission, vision, and values we hold ourselves to, and that we invite Vancouver to hold us to.

01

Mission

To advance AI literacy, inform policy, and promote the safe, sustainable, and ethical development of artificial intelligence across Vancouver.

02

Vision

A Vancouver where safety, sustainability, and ethics sit at the centre of every AI decision, and where every resident has the literacy to hold that standard.

03

Values

  1. i.

    Safety first.

    AI should protect people, communities, and the natural world. Not just today, but for future generations.

  2. ii.

    Sustainability always.

    Energy use, water use, and carbon impact belong in every AI conversation.

  3. iii.

    Ethics without exception.

    We ask not only what can be built, but what should be built and under what conditions.

  4. iv.

    Transparency by default.

    Our research, positions, and reasoning are open.

  5. v.

    Community-rooted.

    We serve Vancouver first, and scale from local depth.

04

Who We Serve

  • 01 The general public
  • 02 Youth and students
  • 03 Industry and developers
  • 04 Policymakers and government
