ABOUT VCASSE

Who We Are

VCASSE is Vancouver's dedicated centre for responsible artificial intelligence. We exist to make sure Vancouver isn't just a participant in the AI conversation. We're helping lead it.

Our Mission

To advance AI literacy, inform policy, and promote the safe, sustainable, and ethical development of artificial intelligence across Vancouver.

Our Vision

A Vancouver where safety, sustainability, and ethics are at the centre of every AI decision, and where every resident has the literacy to hold that standard.

Our Values

Safety first. AI should protect people, communities, and the natural world, not just today but for future generations.

Sustainability always. We bring energy use, water use, and carbon impact into every AI conversation.

Ethics without exception. We ask not only what can be built, but what should be built and under what conditions.

Transparency by default. Our research, positions, and reasoning are open.

Community-rooted. We serve Vancouver first, and scale from local depth.

Who We Serve

The general public, youth and students, tech companies and developers, and policymakers and government.

Affiliation

VCASSE operates as a subsidiary program of Oasis of Change, Inc., a federal not-for-profit corporation based in Vancouver, BC.
