FAQ

Common questions

Quick answers before you write — if anything here doesn't fit your situation, please send us a message.

How quickly will I get a reply?

Most enquiries receive a first response within two business days. Media enquiries are flagged for same-day attention when possible.

Can VCASSE speak at our event or panel?

Yes — we accept speaking requests across safety, sustainability, and ethics topics. Use the form above and select Speaking request, and please include the date, format, and audience.

How do I propose a research collaboration?

Reach out via research@vcasse.org with a short note describing the question, the data or partner involved, and the timeline. We'll set up a 30-minute scoping call.

Are you taking on student researchers or interns?

We work with student researchers from Vancouver-area universities on a rolling basis. Send your CV and a paragraph on what you'd like to work on.

Where can I read your published work?

All briefs, explainers, and research notes are on the publications page, filterable by pillar, tag, and date.
