A few common questions

How quickly will I hear back?

Usually within a couple of business days. Press queries we try to answer same-day.

Can someone from VCASSE speak at our event?

Often, yes. Use the form, pick "Speaking request" from the dropdown, and tell us the date, format, and audience.

How do I propose a research collaboration?

Email research@vcasse.org with the question you're working on, who else is involved, and rough timing. We'll set up a short call from there.

Do you take on student researchers?

Yes, on a rolling basis, mostly with students at Vancouver-area schools. Send a CV and a paragraph on what you'd want to dig into.

Where can I read what you've published?

It's all on the publications page, with filters by topic and tag.
