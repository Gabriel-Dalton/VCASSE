Focus Areas

Three pillars of responsible AI

Our work spans technical safety, environmental and social sustainability, and the ethical questions that shape who benefits from AI and who bears the risk.

Safety

Robustness, alignment, and risk-aware deployment so systems behave reliably in the real world.

Sustainability

Energy, data, and lifecycle impacts of AI systems in a changing climate and economy.
