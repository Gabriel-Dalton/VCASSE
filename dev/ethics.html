PILLAR 03

Ethics & Policy

Ethics is where the hard questions live. Not "can we do this?" but "what happens when we do?" VCASSE's ethics work operates as a think tank, examining the boundaries of AI applications and holding builders accountable.

Ethical Boundaries

Examining the ethical boundaries of AI applications, from image manipulation and deepfakes to surveillance and algorithmic bias. The question isn't just "can we build this?" It's "should we?"

03.2

Corporate Accountability

Holding corporations accountable for how they deploy AI, and recognizing the companies that get it right, including those choosing not to offer certain AI capabilities for ethical reasons.

  • CORPORATE RESPONSIBILITY
  • DEPLOYMENT STANDARDS
03.3

Policy & Positions

Publishing positions and recommendations that help policymakers, developers, and the public navigate ethical grey areas. Safety asks: "Is this AI system built responsibly?" Ethics asks: "Should this AI system exist at all?"
